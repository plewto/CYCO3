CUE Cheatsheet

The image shows arguments to the default BAR function for common note positions.

The time specifications are a three element list: 

    (bar-number beat-number subbeat-number)

Bars 1 through 5 on the top row illustrates specifications for non-modified notes.

Bars 6 through 9 show specifications for triplets. Triplets are indicated by a 'T' suffix to either the beat or subbeat value.

This illustration assumes a default 4/4 time signature with 4 subbeats to the beat and 6 tsubbeats to the beat.
