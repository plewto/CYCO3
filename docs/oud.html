Oud Plugin

The Oud  plugin  is a model of a fretted instrument neck for defining alternate chord-models.  Oud definitions are are in the CYCO-OUD package with selected definitions imported into the main CYCO package.

Oud defines the following classes and structures:

  • CHORD-VARIATIONS  holds all variations of a specific chord-type for a single key.  IE all C Major chords.
  • CHORD-FAMILY  holds all variations of specific chord-type for all keys.  IE all Major chords in all keys.
  • MONOCHORD  defines key-numbers in terms of fret positions on a single string.
  • POLYCHORD  a suite of monochords which extends ABSTRACT-CHORD-MODEL.

Specific instruments are defined by creating an instance of polychord and then defining the possible chords it may produce.  The currently (12.24.2018) defined Oud based instruments are listed below together with a list of chords each instrument produces.  Additional chord type may be added to the current set.

Bass Guitar

The bass-chords plugin define chords for a 4-string bass guitar using standard tuning.  The plugin exports *BASS-CHORD-MODEL* to the CYCO package.  The following chord-types are defined:

[SOLO]   - Single notes
[OCT]    - Octaves
[POWER]  - Root & 5th
[POWER2] - Root, 5th & Octave
[PER4]   - Perfect 4th
[MAJ]    - Major
[7]      - Dominate 7th
[MIN]    - Minor

Guitar

The guitar-chords plugin define chords for a 6-string guitar with standard tuning.  The plugin exports *GUITAR-CHORD-MODEL* to the CYCO package. 

[SOLO]      - Single notes            [7+5]       - Dom 7 sharp 5
[OCT]       - Octaves                 [7+9]       - Dom 7 sharp 9
[POWER2]    - Root & 5th              [7-9]       - Dom 7 flat 9
[POWER]     - Root, 5th & octave      [7-SUS4]    - Dom 7 suspended 4th
[MAJ]       - Major                   [9]         - Dom 9th
[MAJ-SLASH] - Inverted major          [9-SUS4]    - Dom 9 suspended 4th
[MAJ6]      - Major 6th               [11]        - Dom 11th
[MAJ7]      - Major 7th               [13]        - Dom 13th
[MAJ9]      - Major 9th               [AUG]       - Augmented
[ADD9]      - Major add 9             [DIM7]      - Diminished 7th
[MAJ11]     - Major 11th              [DIM]       - Diminished
[MAJ13]     - Major 13th              [MIN]       - Minor
[7]         - Dominate 7th            [MIN+7]     - Minor sharp-7
[7+5+9]     - Dom 7 sharp 5 sharp 9   [MIN-ADD9]  - Minor add 9th
[7+5-9]     - Dom 7 sharp 5 flat 9    [MIN-SLASH] - Inverted minor
[MIN6]      - Minor 6th
[MIN7]      - Minor 7th
[MIN7-5]    - Minor 7th flat 5
[SUS2]      - Suspend 2
[SUS4]      - Suspend 4

Mandolin

The mandolin-chords plugin defines chords for a 4-string mandolin tuned in 5ths.  The plugin exports *MANDOLIN-CHORD-MODEL* to the CYCO package.

[SOLO]   - Single note 
[OCT]    - Octaves
[POWER]  - Root & 5th
[MAJ]    - Major
[MAJ7]   - Major 7th
[MAJ9]   - Major 9th
[69]     - Dominate 6/9
[6]      - Dominate 6th
[7]      - Dominate 7th 
[7+5]    - Dominate 7th sharp-5  
[7-5]    - Dominate 7th flat-5
[7-9]    - Dominate 7th flat-9 
[9]      - Dominate 9th
[DIM]    - Diminished
[MIN]    - Minor
[MIN6]   - Minor 6th
[MIN7]   - Minor 7th
[MIN+7]  - Minor sharp-7
[MIN7-5] - Minor 7th flat-5
[MIN9]   - Minor 9th
[SUS4]   - Suspended 4th

Ukulele

The ukulele-chords plugin defines chords for a 4-string ukulele tuned G C E A.
The plugin exports *UKULELE-CHORD-MODEL* to the CYCO package.

[SOLO] - Single notes
[MAJ]  - Major
[MAJ7] - Major 7th
[6]    - Dominate 6th
[7]    - Dominate 7th
[9]    - Dominate 9th
[MIN]  - Minor
[MIN7] - Minor 7th
[AUG]  - Augmented
[DIM]  - Diminished
